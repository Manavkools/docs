## Overview

The Warranty & Service Reminder Agent proactively reminds customers about upcoming warranty expirations and scheduled service appointments.

## Use Cases

- Automotive service reminders
- Electronics warranty
- Home appliance maintenance
- HVAC service scheduling

## Key Features

- Warranty expiration reminders
- Service appointment scheduling
- Maintenance reminders
- Answer questions
- Schedule service calls
- Update customer records

## Overview

The Survey & Feedback Agent conducts structured surveys and collects valuable customer feedback through voice conversations.

## Use Cases

- Product teams gathering feedback
- Customer success surveys
- Market research
- Post-purchase surveys
- Satisfaction surveys

## Key Features

- Structured survey questions
- Natural conversation flow
- Sentiment analysis
- Response collection
- Follow-up questions
- Data export and analysis

## Overview

The Renewal Reminder Agent proactively contacts customers before contract or subscription renewals, ensuring smooth renewals and reducing churn.

## Use Cases

- SaaS subscription renewals
- Insurance policy renewals
- Membership renewals
- Service contract renewals

## Key Features

- Proactive renewal reminders
- Answer renewal questions
- Process renewals
- Handle objections
- Update payment information
- Confirm renewal details

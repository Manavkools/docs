## Overview

The Customer Health Check Agent proactively reaches out to assess customer satisfaction, identify risks, and ensure successful outcomes.

## Use Cases

- B2B SaaS customer success
- Enterprise software accounts
- Managed services
- High-value customer relationships

## Key Features

- Health score assessment
- Usage and engagement tracking
- Risk identification
- Satisfaction measurement
- Upsell opportunities
- Churn prevention

## Overview

The Training & Adoption Agent helps users learn and adopt your product through personalized training conversations.

## Use Cases

- Software companies onboarding users
- Technology product training
- Enterprise tool adoption
- Feature education

## Key Features

- Personalized training paths
- Feature walkthroughs
- Answer questions
- Track progress
- Schedule follow-up sessions
- Measure adoption

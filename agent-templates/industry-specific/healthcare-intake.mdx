## Overview

The Healthcare Patient Intake Agent handles patient intake calls, collecting medical history and scheduling appointments.

## Use Cases

- Hospitals
- Medical clinics
- Diagnostic centers
- Telehealth services

## Key Features

- Patient information collection
- Medical history
- Appointment scheduling
- Insurance verification
- HIPAA compliance
- Secure data handling

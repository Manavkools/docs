## Overview

The Gym & Fitness Membership Agent handles membership inquiries, bookings, and member communications for fitness facilities.

## Use Cases

- Gyms
- Fitness studios
- Yoga centers
- Sports facilities

## Key Features

- Membership information
- Class bookings
- Personal training scheduling
- Membership renewals
- Answer questions
- Member communications

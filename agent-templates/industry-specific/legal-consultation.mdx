## Overview

The Legal Consultation Scheduler Agent handles consultation requests and schedules appointments for legal practices.

## Use Cases

- Law firms
- Legal aid organizations
- Corporate legal departments
- Legal service providers

## Key Features

- Consultation scheduling
- Case information collection
- Attorney matching
- Answer questions
- Confidentiality handling
- Appointment confirmations

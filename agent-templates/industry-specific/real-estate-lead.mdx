## Overview

The Real Estate Lead Qualifier Agent screens and qualifies real estate leads, collecting essential information before connecting with agents.

## Use Cases

- Real estate agencies
- Property developers
- Real estate brokerages
- Property management companies

## Key Features

- Lead qualification questions
- Property preferences
- Budget and timeline
- Schedule property viewings
- Connect with agents
- Update CRM

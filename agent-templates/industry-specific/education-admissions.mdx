## Overview

The Education Admissions Agent assists prospective students with admissions inquiries, applications, and information.

## Use Cases

- Universities
- Colleges
- Training institutes
- Online education platforms

## Key Features

- Admissions information
- Application assistance
- Answer questions
- Schedule campus tours
- Program information
- Application status

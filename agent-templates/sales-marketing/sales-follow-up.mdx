## Overview

The Sales Follow-Up Agent automates follow-up conversations with prospects, nurturing leads and moving them through the sales funnel.

## Use Cases

- B2B sales teams
- Real estate agencies
- Insurance brokers
- Financial services
- Professional services

## Key Features

- Personalized follow-up based on previous interactions
- Answer product/service questions
- Schedule meetings and demos
- Handle objections
- Qualify leads further
- Update CRM automatically

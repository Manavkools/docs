## Overview

The Win-Back Campaign Agent reaches out to churned customers with personalized offers and incentives to return.

## Use Cases

- Subscription businesses
- SaaS platforms
- Membership programs
- Telecom companies
- Service providers

## Key Features

- Identify churn reasons
- Present win-back offers
- Address concerns
- Re-activate subscriptions
- Collect feedback
- Update customer status

## Overview

The Product Launch Announcement Agent proactively informs customers about new products, features, or updates through personalized voice calls.

## Use Cases

- SaaS companies launching new features
- E-commerce new product announcements
- Subscription service updates
- Service enhancements

## Key Features

- Personalized product announcements
- Feature highlights and benefits
- Answer questions about new products
- Schedule demos or consultations
- Collect feedback
- Drive adoption

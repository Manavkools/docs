## How to Use Templates

1. **Browse Templates**: Explore templates by category or use case
2. **Select Template**: Choose the template that matches your needs
3. **Customize**: Adjust prompts, voice, and settings for your brand
4. **Test**: Run test conversations in the Tabbly Playground
5. **Deploy**: Activate for live calls once satisfied

## Customization Options

All templates are fully customizable:
- Conversation flow and prompts
- Voice and personality
- Business logic and workflows
- Integrations and data collection
- Language and localization

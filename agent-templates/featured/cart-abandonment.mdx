## Overview

The Cart Abandonment Recovery Agent proactively reaches out to customers who have abandoned their shopping carts, helping recover lost sales through personalized voice conversations.

## Use Cases

- **E-commerce**: Recover abandoned online shopping carts
- **Retail**: Follow up on in-store inquiries
- **Online Services**: Re-engage potential customers
- **Subscription Services**: Convert trial users

## Key Features

- Automatic cart abandonment detection
- Personalized outreach based on cart contents
- Special offers and discounts
- Answer product questions
- Complete purchase over phone
- Multi-channel follow-up

## Conversation Flow

1. **Greeting**: Friendly, non-intrusive approach
2. **Acknowledge Cart**: Reference specific items
3. **Address Concerns**: Answer questions or concerns
4. **Offer Assistance**: Help complete purchase
5. **Special Offers**: Present discounts if applicable
6. **Complete Purchase**: Process order if ready
7. **Follow-up**: Confirm order and next steps

## Customization Options

- Cart value thresholds
- Timing of outreach calls
- Discount offers
- Product information
- Brand voice and tone
- Multi-language support

## Integration Options

- E-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento)
- Payment processors
- Inventory systems
- CRM systems
- Email marketing tools

## Overview

The Appointment Scheduling Agent automates the entire appointment booking process, from initial contact to calendar confirmation. Perfect for businesses that need to manage high volumes of appointment requests efficiently.

## Use Cases

- **Medical Clinics**: Schedule patient appointments, confirmations, and reminders
- **Dental Offices**: Book cleanings, consultations, and procedures
- **Salons & Spas**: Manage beauty appointments and services
- **Professional Services**: Schedule consultations and meetings
- **Fitness Centers**: Book personal training and classes

## Key Features

- Natural conversation flow for booking
- Calendar integration (Google Calendar, Outlook, Calendly)
- Automatic confirmation emails/SMS
- Reminder calls before appointments
- Rescheduling and cancellation handling
- Waitlist management
- Multi-location support

## Conversation Flow

1. **Greeting**: Warm, professional greeting
2. **Service Selection**: Identify desired service or provider
3. **Date Selection**: Find available dates
4. **Time Selection**: Choose preferred time slot
5. **Information Collection**: Gather contact details
6. **Confirmation**: Confirm appointment details
7. **Next Steps**: Provide confirmation and reminders

## Customization Options

- Service types and descriptions
- Available time slots
- Provider preferences
- Cancellation policies
- Reminder preferences
- Multi-language support

## Integration Options

- Calendly
- Custom calendar systems

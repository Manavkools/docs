## Overview

The Recruitment Screening Agent conducts initial phone screenings with job candidates, collecting essential information and qualifying candidates before human recruiter involvement.

## Use Cases

- **HR Departments**: Screen candidates for open positions
- **Recruitment Agencies**: Qualify candidates for clients
- **High-Volume Hiring**: Handle large applicant volumes
- **Temporary Staffing**: Quick screening for temp positions

## Key Features

- Structured screening questions
- Qualification scoring
- Availability and scheduling
- Salary expectations
- Experience verification
- Automated scheduling for interviews
- Candidate ranking and prioritization

## Conversation Flow

1. **Introduction**: Professional greeting and position overview
2. **Basic Qualifications**: Verify minimum requirements
3. **Experience Review**: Discuss relevant experience
4. **Availability**: Confirm schedule and availability
5. **Compensation**: Discuss salary expectations
6. **Interest Level**: Gauge candidate interest
7. **Next Steps**: Schedule interview or provide feedback

## Customization Options

- Job-specific questions
- Qualification criteria
- Interview scheduling
- Rejection messaging
- Multi-language support
- Industry-specific requirements

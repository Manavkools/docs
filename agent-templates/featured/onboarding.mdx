## Overview

The Onboarding Agent guides new customers through the onboarding process, ensuring they understand your product and get started successfully.

## Use Cases

- **SaaS Platforms**: Onboard new users
- **Subscription Services**: Welcome new subscribers
- **Membership Programs**: Guide new members
- **Digital Products**: Help users get started

## Key Features

- Welcome and introduction
- Product overview and features
- Setup assistance
- Account configuration
- Training and tutorials
- Success metrics tracking
- Follow-up scheduling

## Conversation Flow

1. **Welcome**: Warm welcome and congratulations
2. **Introduction**: Overview of product/service
3. **Needs Assessment**: Understand customer goals
4. **Setup Guide**: Walk through initial setup
5. **Feature Highlights**: Key features and benefits
6. **Training Resources**: Provide learning materials
7. **Next Steps**: Schedule follow-up if needed

## Customization Options

- Product-specific information
- Onboarding steps
- Success criteria
- Follow-up timing
- Multi-language support
- Industry-specific guidance

## Integration Options

- Product platforms
- Learning management systems
- CRM systems
- Email marketing tools
- Analytics platforms

## Overview

Voice settings are crucial for creating natural, engaging conversations. Tabbly offers extensive voice customization options including voice library selection, custom voice cloning, and multi-language support.

## Import Voices from Library

### Voice Library

Tabbly's voice library includes hundreds of high-quality voices across multiple languages, accents, and styles.

**Accessing the Library**
1. Navigate to **Agent Settings > Voice**
2. Click **Browse Voice Library**
3. Filter by language, gender, age, or style
4. Preview voices by listening to samples
5. Select your preferred voice

### Voice Categories

**Gender Options**
- Male voices
- Female voices
- Gender-neutral options

**Age Ranges**
- Young adult (20-30)
- Adult (30-50)
- Mature (50+)

**Styles**
- Professional
- Friendly
- Authoritative
- Conversational
- Energetic

### Previewing Voices

- **Sample Phrases**: Listen to standard phrases
- **Custom Text**: Test with your own text
- **Conversation Samples**: Hear full conversation examples
- **Side-by-Side Comparison**: Compare multiple voices

## Clone Voice (Custom Voice Cloning)

### What is Voice Cloning?

Voice cloning allows you to create a custom voice that matches your brand, spokesperson, or specific requirements.

### Requirements

**Audio Requirements**
- **Duration**: Minimum 30 minutes of clean audio
- **Quality**: High-quality recording (16kHz+ sample rate)
- **Format**: WAV, MP3, or M4A
- **Content**: Natural speech, varied sentences

**Best Practices for Recording**
- Use a professional microphone
- Record in a quiet environment
- Speak naturally and clearly
- Include varied sentence types
- Maintain consistent tone and pace

### Cloning Process

**Step 1: Prepare Audio**
- Record or collect audio samples
- Ensure high quality and clarity
- Remove background noise
- Normalize audio levels

**Step 2: Upload Audio**
- Upload audio files to Tabbly
- System validates quality and duration
- Processing begins automatically

**Step 3: Training**
- AI model trains on your audio
- Process typically takes 24-48 hours
- You'll receive notification when complete

**Step 4: Testing**
- Test cloned voice in Playground
- Refine if needed
- Deploy to agents

### Use Cases

- **Brand Voice**: Match your brand's spokesperson
- **Multilingual**: Clone voice in multiple languages
- **Consistency**: Maintain consistent voice across all agents
- **Personalization**: Use specific voices for different scenarios

## Multi-Language Voice Selection

### Supported Languages

Tabbly supports 30+ languages including:
- **English**: US, UK, Australian, Indian variants
- **Spanish**: Spain, Latin America variants
- **French**: France, Canadian variants
- **German**: Standard German
- **Hindi**: Indian Hindi
- **Tamil, Telugu, Kannada**: South Indian languages
- **Arabic**: Modern Standard Arabic
- **Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean**: And many more

### Language Selection

**Primary Language**
- Set the main language for your agent
- Most conversations will use this language
- Default voice selected for primary language

**Additional Languages**
- Add support for multiple languages
- Automatic language detection
- Seamless language switching during calls

### Automatic Language Detection

- **Detects Caller Language**: Identifies caller's preferred language
- **Switches Automatically**: Agent responds in detected language
- **Maintains Context**: Conversation continues smoothly
- **Fallback Options**: Defaults to primary language if detection fails

## Testing Different Voices

### Voice Testing Tools

**Playground Testing**
- Test voices in realistic scenarios
- Compare multiple voices side-by-side
- Evaluate naturalness and clarity
- Test with your actual conversation flows

**A/B Testing**
- Test different voices with real callers
- Compare performance metrics
- Measure customer satisfaction
- Choose best-performing voice

### Evaluation Criteria

**Naturalness**
- Does it sound human-like?
- Are there any robotic artifacts?
- Is the pacing natural?

**Clarity**
- Is speech easy to understand?
- Are words pronounced correctly?
- Is there good articulation?

**Appropriateness**
- Does it match your brand?
- Is it suitable for your use case?
- Does it resonate with your audience?

**Performance**
- How do callers respond?
- What's the conversion rate?
- What's the customer satisfaction?

## Voice Configuration Options

### Speech Rate

**Adjustable Speed**
- **Slow**: 0.8x normal speed
- **Normal**: 1.0x normal speed
- **Fast**: 1.2x normal speed
- **Custom**: Set specific rate

### Pitch and Tone

**Pitch Adjustment**
- Higher pitch for energy
- Lower pitch for authority
- Natural pitch for balance

**Tone Settings**
- Warm and friendly
- Professional and formal
- Energetic and enthusiastic
- Calm and reassuring

### Pauses and Emphasis

**Natural Pauses**
- Configure pause duration
- Add pauses for emphasis
- Natural breathing patterns

**Emphasis Points**
- Mark important words/phrases
- Adjust emphasis intensity
- Improve message clarity

## **What is the Agent Templates Library?**

The Tabbly Agent Templates Library offers production-ready voice AI agents that you can deploy in minutes. Each template comes pre-configured with:

- **Optimized Prompts**: Expert-crafted conversation flows tested across thousands of calls
- **Smart Workflows**: Pre-built logic for common business processes
- **Best Practice Settings**: Ideal voice, language, and processing configurations
- **Customizable Variables**: Easy personalization for your brand and use case

Instead of building from scratch, start with a proven template and customize it to your specific requirements.

## **How to Use Agent Templates?**

**Quick Start in 5 Steps:**

1. **Browse & Select**: Find the template that matches your use case below
2. **Import to Dashboard**: Click "Use This Template" to add it to your workspace
3. **Customize**: Adjust prompts, voice, and settings to match your brand
4. **Test**: Run conversations in the Tabbly Playground to refine behavior
5. **Deploy**: Activate for live calls once you're satisfied with performance

Each template is fully editable—treat it as your starting point, not a limitation.

## **Template Customization Guide**

Every template can be tailored to your specific needs:

### **What You Can Customize**

**Conversation Flow**

- Opening greeting and introduction
- Questions and qualification criteria
- Objection handling responses
- Closing and next steps

**Voice & Personality**

- Voice selection (male/female, accent, tone)
- Speaking pace and style
- Formality level
- Brand-specific phrases

**Business Logic**

- Integrations with your CRM, calendar, or database
- Custom data collection fields
- Conditional routing rules
- Post-call automation triggers

**Language & Localization**

- Primary and fallback languages
- Regional dialects and accents
- Time zone and business hours
- Currency and measurement formats

### **Customization Best Practices**

1. **Start Small**: Import the template as-is and test it first
2. **Iterative Changes**: Make one change at a time and test the impact
3. **Use Variables**: Leverage dynamic fields for personalization
4. **Test Thoroughly**: Run multiple test conversations before going live
5. **Monitor & Refine**: Review real call data and continuously improve

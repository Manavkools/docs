## Choosing the Right Plan

### Start with Starter if:
- You're new to voice AI
- You have low call volumes (< 1,000 minutes/month)
- You need basic features
- You want to test the platform

### Upgrade to Professional if:
- You're scaling your operations
- You need 10,000+ call minutes/month
- You require advanced analytics
- You need priority support

### Consider Enterprise if:
- You have high-volume requirements
- You need custom features
- You require HIPAA compliance
- You need dedicated support and SLA guarantees

## Overview

Tabbly offers flexible, usage-based pricing designed to scale with your business. Our pricing model ensures you only pay for what you use while providing access to powerful features that help you automate and enhance your voice communications.

## Pricing Components

Tabbly pricing is based on several key factors:

### Call Minutes
- **Inbound Calls**: Charged per minute of conversation
- **Outbound Calls**: Charged per minute of conversation
- **Transcription**: Included with all call minutes
- **Recording Storage**: Included up to your plan limits

### Agent Deployment
- **Active Agents**: Number of agents you can deploy simultaneously
- **Agent Templates**: Access to pre-built templates
- **Custom Agents**: Ability to create unlimited custom agents

### Features & Integrations
- **Basic Features**: Included in all plans
- **Advanced Features**: Available on higher tiers
- **CRM Integrations**: Included or available as add-ons
- **API Access**: Available on all plans

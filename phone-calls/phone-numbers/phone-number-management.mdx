## Overview

Phone number management is essential for setting up your voice AI operations. Tabbly provides comprehensive tools for purchasing, configuring, and managing phone numbers.

## How to Buy Phone Numbers

### Purchasing Process

**Step 1: Access Number Marketplace**
- Navigate to **Settings > Phone Numbers**
- Click **Buy New Number**
- Browse available numbers

**Step 2: Select Number Type**
- **Local Numbers**: Area code specific
- **Toll-Free Numbers**: 800, 888, 877, etc.
- **International Numbers**: Global coverage

**Step 3: Choose Location**
- Select country
- Choose area code or city
- Search for specific number patterns

**Step 4: Purchase Number**
- Review pricing
- Confirm selection
- Complete purchase
- Number activated immediately

### Number Types

**Local Numbers**
- Area code specific
- Lower cost
- Local presence
- Best for regional operations

**Toll-Free Numbers**
- 800, 888, 877, 866, 855, 844
- Free for callers
- Professional image
- Higher cost

**International Numbers**
- Global coverage
- Local presence in other countries
- Support for international calling
- Varies by country

## Porting Existing Numbers to Tabbly

### Porting Process

**Step 1: Initiate Port Request**
- Navigate to **Phone Numbers > Port Number**
- Enter number to port
- Provide account information

**Step 2: Submit Documentation**
- Letter of Authorization (LOA)
- Recent phone bill
- Account verification

**Step 3: Port Approval**
- Tabbly reviews request
- Carrier processes port
- Typically 7-14 business days

**Step 4: Port Completion**
- Number activated on Tabbly
- Test call to verify
- Update any external systems

### Porting Requirements

**Eligibility**
- Number must be active
- Account must be in good standing
- Must be portable (not all numbers are)

**Documentation Needed**
- Letter of Authorization
- Recent phone bill
- Account number and PIN
- Business verification (if applicable)

## Vanity Number Options

### What are Vanity Numbers?

Vanity numbers are phone numbers that spell words or are easy to remember (e.g., 1-800-FLOWERS).
